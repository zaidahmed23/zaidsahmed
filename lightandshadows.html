An Ode to the Sun

A look at the relationship between me, shadows, and sun, as well as my (unhealthy) need for sunlight.


A day well spent with family and ended in the best way possible- in the sun.





A view from waking up for Fajr- twilight sun reflecting in the bedroom.


Riding through the city as the sun goes down- Houston travels.


Family room during golden hour- sitting in the sun seems to make all your problems go away.





September weather is perfect for sitting outside, soaking up the sun- even in Boston.


Evening walk at the beginning of quarantine- what seems like a lifetime ago.




Just one of Baba's plants throughout the house- lighting makes it look ethereal.





Listening to "Warm Glow" by Hippocampus- a perfect encapsulation for this photo.


Listening to anything Sampha- a true artist in every sense of the word.


A silhouette during golden hour from the top floor of a hotel- can't get any better.


Doing anything to get a little bit of sun- I can feel my serotonin increase.


The green hits different when the sun is beaming on it- just one of the many things in life that is better with the sun.
