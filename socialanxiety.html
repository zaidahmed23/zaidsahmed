Social Anxiety

Poem by me about my experience with social anxiety


Today I closed myself in
and weeped tears of sadness
and loneliness
No one is aware
and yet life must go on ​
Walking down the street
seeing imaginary eyes on me
hearing imaginary voices talking about me
My heart beating out of my chest
My hands trembling out of fear
making even the simplest of tasks
seem like moving a mountain
No one understands
and yet life must go on
I will overcome
Maybe not today
Maybe not tomorrow ​
But someday
