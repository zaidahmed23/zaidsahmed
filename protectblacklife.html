Protect Black Life

Poem written by me: 24 lines, 8 syllables each


How much is black life really worth,
when they are killed for the simple
fact that they were in their own home,
they were playing with a toy in
public, they were jogging, ​they had
a hoodie on, they were selling
cigarettes, or just the fact that
they are black, In a country that
was built on their very backs four
centuries ago. A country that ​
criminalizes black people on
the sole basis of them being
black. A country that claims it gives
liberty and justice for all
even though it still has not ended
slavery as it conducts it
through a new avenue that is
the prison industrial complex.
A country that still has not paid
reparations or even came
close to acknowledging the four
centuries of oppression that
they have caused. This is a country
that has never gave real justice.
